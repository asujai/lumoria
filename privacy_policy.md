# Privacy Policy for Context PDF

**Last Updated:** [Date]

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is collected, used, and explicitly NOT shared when you use the Context PDF application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Context PDF is a privacy-first application.**
We do not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or share your personal data, PDF documents, or reading habits.

- **Local Storage:** All PDF files you open, the text snippets you analyze, and your application settings (such as Dark Mode or selected Language) are stored **locally on your device**.
- **API Key Security:** The API Key you provide (e.g., Google Gemini API Key) is stored securely on your device using encrypted local storage (`flutter_secure_storage`). We do not have access to this key. It is only used to directly communicate from your device to the AI service provider.

## 2. Third-Party Services

To provide AI analysis features, your device communicates directly with the API provider you configure (e.g., Google Gemini).

- When you select text for analysis, only the selected snippet and the surrounding context (for better results) are sent directly to the AI provider.
- For information on how the AI provider handles your data, please refer to their respective Privacy Policy (e.g., [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)).

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
